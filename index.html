About Skillexa

Skillexa About Skillexa Skillexa is India’s first AI-powered skill certification platform that empowers individuals by validating real-world skills—beyond just degrees. Whether you're mastering Communication, Public Speaking, Decision Making, or Teamwork, Skillexa recognizes what truly matters in today’s job market. We use intelligent AI systems to assess and certify your soft skills and critical thinking abilities, offering instant digital certificates starting at just ₹99. Our certifications are designed with industry standards in mind and are recognized by hiring professionals and companies looking for more than just academic scores.

Whether you're preparing for interviews, upgrading your soft skills, or exploring new fields, Skillexa is your companion on a journey of self-growth and success. Our mission is simple: Make learning accessible, human-like, and impactful for everyone.
